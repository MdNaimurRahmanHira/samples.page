Analytic Report

Samples

To help you to get started we have created a range of reports that any subscriber can use straight “out of the box”.

They samples show how easy it is to create powerful analytic reports with Monitor+. The reports below vary from the simple to the very complex.

Sample analytic files.

With R, Python, Jupyter and Monitor+, any analytics can be applied to your market data and ad-hoc analytic reports can be created quickly and easily. Averages, correlations, seasonality, curves, ARIMA, machine learning, forecasting and thousands more are readily available. There are no limits to the number of reports you can create - you can even integrate your own data.

Give your traders and analysts a commercial advantage by helping them to better analyse the markets with custom analytic reports.


The sample reports below are designed to get you creating useful reports immediately. Any subscribers can download the reports and adapt them to their own requirements. More reports will be added and if you’ve created a report that one you’d like to share on this page then let us know. We’d love to hear from you.
